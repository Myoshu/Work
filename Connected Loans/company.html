About Us

Connected Loans is an Australian owned business that was created to provide home owners with real choice on how they experience their home loan journey.
We offer people then choice of getting the home loan online from the comfort of their home, making an appointment to chat with someone over the phone or by booking an appointment and meeting some face to face… the choice is yours on how you can get access to our panel of over 40 lenders.
We are available to talk anyway you like… we are available on Skype, webchat or via phone anytime you want.
So what are you waiting for? Get your next home loan now with Connected Loans.
